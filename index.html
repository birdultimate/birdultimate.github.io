About Bird

Bird is an ultimate frisbee club from Minneapolis, Minnesota. Bird competes in the Select Flight of the USA Ultimate Mixed Club Series.

Founded in 2012, Bird brought together various groups of talented Minnesota players, many with Carleton, St. Olaf or South High School roots. In that inaugural season, Bird would compile a 20-9 record, finishing 5th at North Central Regionals. In 2013, the team would compete in its first Regional final en route to a 3rd place finish, and hold the dubious honor of being the only team to defeat eventual National Champion Drag'n Thrust in the USAU Series.

In 2014, Bird traveled to Colorado for the first time and came out strong against highly competitive teams. We had a strong finish at Regionals, battling injuries that decimated our roster, we fought hard to finish 4th behind Drag'n Thrust, CLX, and NOISE.

The team has high expectations coming into the 2015 season, looking to become even more competitive and raise its profile on the national level. We would like to see additional bids to our region. Having back-to-back National Champions and World Champions, (both Drag'n and CLX have won the World Champions) in our Region creates unique challenges for Bird, but ultimately makes us stronger competitors. Bird is on an upward trajectory of success and looks to the 2015 season as a pivotal point for making things happen.
